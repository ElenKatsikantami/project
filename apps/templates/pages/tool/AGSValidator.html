AGS Validator


  • info Description
  • timeline Validate
  • gavel Reference

This is an easy-to-use on-line AGS validation tool. Upload your .ags files to review and verify their format and summarize content. This tool is in line with guidelines from the Association of Geotechnical & Geoenvironmental Specialists and the published AGS validator application (Beta). Note that the validator only checks the format of the files and not accuracy of the data itself.

The content of the AGS is summarized in terms of general information for the ags file, borehole information, field, laboratory and chemical testing information.

Works for AGS 3.0,4.0 or 4.1 formats in line with Association of Geotechnical & Geoenvironmental Specialists (AGS.org).
